My Favorite!

Cinnamon roll waffles are absolutely amazing! The combination of the sweet, cinnamon-infused waffle batter and the creamy icing drizzle on top is pure bliss. These waffles are not just a breakfast treat; they're a warm and comforting hug in food form. The smell of cinnamon filling the air as they cook is enough to make anyone's mouth water. They are the perfect indulgence for a lazy weekend morning or a special brunch occasion. I love them because they bring warmth and happiness to my taste buds, and they're a delightful twist on classic waffles. Not to mention it is an easy and delicious recipe to follow at home!

How to make at home!

  • 1 3/4 cups milk
